About

We bring Tubbies in costumes to make every party magical.

From kids’ birthdays to corporate fun days, our playful Tubbies arrive in vibrant costumes to entertain, greet, and pose for photos— turning moments into memories.

Our Story

Born from a love of smiles, built for unforgettable parties.

We started by surprising a few friends and quickly grew into a costumed-entertainment team. Today, our Tubbies bring high‑energy dance, gentle meet‑and‑greets, and photogenic fun to parties of every size. Simple booking, punctual arrivals, and professional handlers keep everything joyful and stress‑free.
